TranscormerMD (Python, PyTorch, CUDA)

Summer 2024

Currently in development while working at the Los Alamos National Labs Theoretical Division, this project aims to apply a score-based diffusion models for predicting protein folding pathways. Score-based diffusion models are SUPER cool because they model generative learning as a continuous-time diffusive process governed by a stochastic differential equation (SDE). That is, if we can accurately predict the final conformations of proteins, as Google's AlphaFold can already do, then the continuous-time model may be suitable for pulling intermediate samples allowing us to extrapolate the entire protein folding pathway.

Mean Reversion/Pairs-Trading Bot (C++)

May 2024

This was a fun exploratory project to gain familiarity with, and excitement for, quantitative trading. For this project, I used CURL to download recent market data from online sources. I then contructed various indicators using Bollinger bands and correlation analysis between the list of stocks. Next, I used Dlib's machine learning tools predict future price movements based on these indicator signals. For risk assessment, I used Monte Carlo simulations to estimate the potential future price paths and assess the risk of different trading strategies. Finally, I dynamically adjust my trading model accoridng to a changepoint detection scheme.
